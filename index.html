MEDLORA

Medlora, was established in March 2022 with a purpose to deliver quality medicine to people who do not want to be hassled on going to a pharmacy. Mr. Quijote, Mr. Delos Reyes, Mr. Giron, Mr. Gadiane, Ms. Norella and Mr. Camat are entrepreneurs who want to develop an application to those people who don’t want to go to pharmacy. Developers enter entrepreneurship to learn more knowledge about businesses and to enhance their programming skill by developing an app

Our company name comes from the word “Medicine and Zalora” which is a major ecommerce site in the Philippines. We combined the word Medicine and Zalora that became Medlora. The name of our company made us realize that we can give out or deliver medicine without the hassle of our consumer going out. Our company logo states the cross logo as to symbolize health and we put our tagline which is “Best Medicine, Best Price” for our future consumer to know what we can give our services. We also used white background to serve as cleanliness as we deliver out the products that our consumer bought.
